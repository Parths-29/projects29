Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris

Arrive at Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to your hotel in the heart of Paris. Check-in and relax. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner at a charming local bistro. Your tour manager will provide an orientation briefing about the days ahead. Overnight in Paris.

Day 2: Paris City Tour & Eiffel Tower

After breakfast, embark on a comprehensive city tour of Paris. See iconic landmarks including the Arc de Triomphe, Champs-Élysées, Place de la Concorde, Notre-Dame Cathedral (exterior view), and the Latin Quarter. After lunch at a local restaurant, visit the Eiffel Tower with skip-the-line tickets to the 2nd floor for panoramic views of the city. In the evening, enjoy a magical Seine River dinner cruise, admiring the illuminated monuments as you savor authentic French cuisine. Overnight in Paris.

Day 3: Louvre Museum & Le Marais

Today, visit the world-famous Louvre Museum with an expert guide to see masterpieces like the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and Winged Victory. After lunch, explore the historic Le Marais district with its trendy boutiques, art galleries, and beautiful Place des Vosges. The evening is free to relax or enjoy optional activities such as a cabaret show at the famous Moulin Rouge (at additional cost). Overnight in Paris.

Day 4: Versailles Palace Day Trip

After breakfast, take a guided day trip to the magnificent Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site and former residence of French kings. Explore the opulent State Apartments, the dazzling Hall of Mirrors, and the beautifully manicured gardens. Return to Paris in the late afternoon. The evening is free to explore at your leisure or join an optional gourmet food tour to discover French culinary specialties. Overnight in Paris.

Day 5: Montmartre & Free Time

This morning, discover the bohemian neighborhood of Montmartre, known for its artistic heritage. Visit the white-domed Sacré-Cœur Basilica and explore the Place du Tertre where artists set up their easels. The afternoon is free for shopping or visiting museums with your Paris Museum Pass. Options include the Musée d'Orsay, Centre Pompidou, or Rodin Museum. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner at a traditional French restaurant with live music. Overnight in Paris.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Depending on your flight time, enjoy some last-minute shopping or leisure time. Transfer to Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport for your return flight with memories of the romantic City of Light.

Visa Information

Indian citizens need a Schengen visa to visit France. The visa processing usually takes 15-20 working days. Our team will assist you with the documentation and application process.

Weather

Paris has a temperate climate with mild summers and cool winters. The best time to visit is from April to October. Spring (April-June) and fall (September-October) offer pleasant temperatures and fewer crowds. Summer temperatures typically range from 15°C to 25°C.

Language & Currency

The official language is French, though English is spoken in major tourist areas and hotels. The currency is the Euro (€). Credit cards are widely accepted, but it's good to carry some cash for small purchases.
