Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for popular destinations, especially during peak travel seasons. This ensures you get the best rates and availability for accommodations and activities.

Are flights included in your packages?

Some of our packages include international flights while others don't. The package details will clearly specify what's included. We can also arrange flights separately if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans to help you spread the cost of your vacation.

Do I need travel insurance?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect against unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, and other unforeseen circumstances during your trip.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy offers a full refund if canceled 60 days before departure, 50% refund if canceled 30-59 days before, and no refund for cancellations less than 30 days before departure. Some packages may have different terms.
