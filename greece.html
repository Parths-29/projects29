Tour Overview

Experience the magic of Santorini, a stunning volcanic island in the Cyclades group of the Greek islands. Known for its spectacular sunsets, blue-domed churches, and whitewashed buildings cascading down the cliffs, Santorini is a dream destination for travelers seeking beauty, romance, and unforgettable experiences.

Highlights

  • Explore the picturesque villages of Oia, Fira, and Imerovigli
  • Witness the world-famous Santorini sunset from Oia
  • Sail around the caldera on a traditional boat cruise
  • Visit ancient ruins at Akrotiri archaeological site
  • Relax on the unique Red Beach and Black Beach
  • Taste authentic Greek cuisine and local Santorini wines
  • Experience the vibrant local culture and traditions

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Santorini

Arrive at Santorini International Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to your hotel. Check-in and relax. Enjoy a welcome dinner at the hotel with wonderful caldera views. Overnight in Santorini.

Day 2: Exploring Fira

After breakfast, join a guided walking tour of Fira, Santorini's capital. Visit the Archaeological Museum and the Museum of Prehistoric Thera. Enjoy free time to wander the charming streets and shop for souvenirs. In the evening, enjoy dinner at a local restaurant. Overnight in Santorini.

Day 3: Oia and Sunset Experience

Morning visit to the beautiful village of Oia, famous for its blue-domed churches and breathtaking views. Explore the narrow streets and enjoy lunch at a local taverna. Free time in the afternoon. Return to Oia in the evening to witness the world-famous Santorini sunset. Dinner at a romantic restaurant with caldera views. Overnight in Santorini.

Day 4: Caldera Sailing Trip

After breakfast, embark on a sailing cruise around the caldera. Visit the volcanic hot springs, swim in crystal-clear waters, and enjoy a BBQ lunch on board. Visit Thirassia island and enjoy spectacular views of Santorini from the sea. Return to the hotel in the late afternoon. Dinner at the hotel. Overnight in Santorini.

Day 5: Ancient Akrotiri & Beach Day

Visit the prehistoric settlement of Akrotiri, often called the "Minoan Pompeii." Learn about the ancient civilization that once thrived here. Afterward, relax at the famous Red Beach and Black Beach. Enjoy a seafood lunch at a beachside taverna. In the afternoon, visit a local winery for a wine tasting experience. Dinner at a traditional Greek restaurant. Overnight in Santorini.

Day 6: Free Day or Optional Activities

Enjoy a free day to explore Santorini at your own pace. Optional activities include cooking classes, photography tours, or hiking from Fira to Oia along the caldera path. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner at a gourmet restaurant with entertainment featuring traditional Greek music and dance. Overnight in Santorini.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Depending on your flight time, enjoy some last-minute shopping or relaxation. Transfer to Santorini International Airport for your return flight with memories to last a lifetime.

Visa Information

Indian citizens need a Schengen visa to visit Greece. The visa processing usually takes 10-15 working days. Our team will assist you with the documentation and application process.

Weather

Santorini has a Mediterranean climate with hot, dry summers and mild winters. The best time to visit is from April to October, with peak season being June to September. Average temperatures range from 15°C to 30°C depending on the month.

Language & Currency

The official language is Greek, but English is widely spoken in tourist areas. The currency is the Euro (€). Credit cards are accepted in most establishments.
